Taylor Getting Started Contact

A non binary, gender neutral OSS Slack App that will coach us into using more inclusive terms.

What does Taylor really do?

Taylor is a Slack bot you can add to any channel in any workspace you have rights to. Taylor will help you and your teammates be more aware on how we talk. There is a predefined list of non inclusive words for now and if any are used, a DM will be sent to the user letting them know how many terms were used and what they were.

I like what Taylor can do, how do I add them to my workspace?

Glad to hear you like Taylor. You can add them to your workspace by clicking the "Add to Slack" button. Once you do that, you'll go through Slack's OAuth process to add to any workspace you're currenly logged into. After that, you'll be prompted to Open in Slack, where you will be able to select only one channel to begin with and click allow. After that, you can click to Open in Slack and the Slack App will automatically open. To add Taylor to the channel, you'll /invite @Taylor, or any other channels if you want.
