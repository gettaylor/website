Taylor Getting Started Contact

Here are the steps on getting started with Taylor. Please click the button below to open Slack in your workspace.

After adding Taylor to your Slack workspace, be sure to '/invite @taylor' to any channels you like.

Taylor will listen to all channels they are added to. If a person uses any of the predefined, non inclusive term(s), Taylor will send a DM to the user. The notification tells them how many non inclusive term(s) were used and also what the term(s) were.
