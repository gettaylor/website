Contact Getting Started

Here are the steps to get started with Taylor. Click the button below to open Slack in your workspace.

After adding Taylor to your Slack workspace.

Invite them to any channel inside your workspace by using /invite @Taylor. Taylor will listen to all channels they are added to. If a full organization member user happens to use any of the predefined, non inclusive term(s), Taylor will send a private message.

Since this project is open source, you can modify which type of users can be notified according to your needs.
